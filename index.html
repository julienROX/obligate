How it reads. Per borrowable reserve: Borrow APR = IRM(utilization) and Supply APY = Borrow APR × utilization × (1 − take rate). A reserve clears at the highest utilization where borrower demand covers the borrowed amount. Market liquidity spreads over borrowable reserves by weight, capped by deposit cap.
