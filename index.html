Profitability & equilibrium

how looper profitability and lender yield are computed

How it reads. Per borrowable reserve: Borrow APR = IRM(utilization) and Lender supply APY = Borrow APR × utilization × (1 − take rate). A looper deposits collateral earning its native yield, borrows the debt asset, and loops to leverage L → looper net APY = native yield × L − borrow APR × (L − 1). Looping pays only while native yield stays above the borrow APR.
