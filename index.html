How it reads. Per borrowable reserve, Borrow APR = IRM(utilization) and Supply APY = Borrow APR × utilization × (1 − take rate). A reserve clears at the highest utilization (bounded by its utilization cap) where borrower demand still covers the borrowed amount. Market liquidity spreads over borrowable reserves by weight, capped by each deposit cap; undeployed capital dilutes the blended Supply APY. Turn override on to pin a reserve's utilization (this shared value also drives the Designer tab).
